Archer Butler was born in Greensboro, North Carolina in 1993 to Wendy and Paul Bulter. Archer spent his entire adolescent life growing up in Jamestown, North Carolina. In 1995, his brother, Tanner Butler was born. Archer grew up going to public schools in Greensboro before graduating from Southwest Guilford High School in 2011. Shortly after Archer graduated from high school, he enlisted in the United States Army as a 11B Infantryman. Archer attended one station unit training (OSUT) at Fort Benning, Georgia before being stationed at Fort Bliss, Texas with the 1st Armored Division as a Bradley crewman in his first unit assignment. While in the Army Archer had assignments to South Korea at Camp Casey, Camp Lemonnier in Djibouti, Africa, and Camp Beuhring, Kuwait in support of Operation Enduring Freedom. While in the Army, Acher achieved the rank of Sergeant before being honorably discharged in 2017.

Post Army Archer moved back to Greensboro to be close to his family again. Archer began working for a pest control company before accepting a managerial position at another company where he currently works. Archer plans on creating his own pest control company in the future and is currently in the planning stages of creation. Archer met his wife, Kasie Butler, in 2018 in Greensboro before getting married to her in 2022. Archer has four dogs, four cats and a fish. Archer enjoys snowboarding, Jeeping and spending time with his friends and family. Archer played rugby for almost 15 years before suffering a knee injury during one of his games. He currently assists his rugby club as a manager and is active in the rugby community.
